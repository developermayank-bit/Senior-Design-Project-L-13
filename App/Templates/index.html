AGRIIFY

Smart Farming for Better Yields and Healthier Crops

AGRIIFY is an innovative smart farming app designed to help farmers make informed decisions about crop selection, fertilizers, and disease analysis. With AGRIIFY, farmers can get personalized recommendations for crops and fertilizers based on their soil type, climate, and location. The app also offers real-time disease analysis, enabling farmers to detect and treat diseases early, preventing damage to their crops.
